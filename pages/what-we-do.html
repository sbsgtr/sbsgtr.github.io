What We
Do

Eight specialised divisions — one integrated approach. From factory floor automation to smart hospitality systems, we deliver turnkey solutions backed by deep technical expertise.

Our Divisions

Complete Industrial
Capabilities

Each division is staffed with certified engineers and technicians who bring hands-on experience from hundreds of successful projects.

Automation & Control

Full-stack industrial automation: PLC programming (Siemens TIA Portal, Allen-Bradley RSLogix, Mitsubishi GX Works), SCADA design (WinCC, Ignition, Citect), pneumatics, HMI, OEE analysis, and complete line retrofits.

  • Siemens S7-1200/1500, Allen-Bradley CompactLogix
  • SCADA with historian and reporting
  • Pneumatic circuit design & installation
  • Legacy system migration & retrofit

CNC & Tool Reselling

End-to-end CNC solutions: fiber laser cutters, lathe tools, cutting inserts, boring bars, milling tools, and CNC retrofit services — sourced from top global brands.

  • Fiber laser cutting machines (1kW–12kW)
  • Turning & milling tooling packages
  • CNC spindle repair & calibration
  • Tooling inventory management

PCB & Electronics

Full lifecycle electronics manufacturing: schematic capture, PCB layout (1–12 layers, 0.1mm track/gap), SMT/THT assembly, board repair, and automated optical inspection. 3–5 day prototype turns.

  • Multi-layer PCB design (Altium, KiCad)
  • SMT assembly with AOI verification
  • Functional testing & firmware loading
  • Reverse engineering & board repair

Structural & Infrastructure

Industrial infrastructure delivery: LV/MV power distribution, compressed air systems, structural steel fabrication, data cabling (Cat6A, fibre optic), and complete automotive workshop builds.

  • LV switchgear & distribution boards
  • Compressed air piping & receiver tanks
  • Fibre backbone & structured cabling
  • Automotive workshop equipment & layout

Professional Acoustics

Design and installation of professional audio systems: 100V line PA, background music, conference room audio, and emergency evacuation systems compliant with BS 5839-8.

  • 100V line PA & zone paging
  • Conference room audio & DSP
  • Emergency voice evacuation
  • Background music & multi-zone

Smart Systems

Intelligent automation for agriculture, homes, kitchens, and energy. From drip irrigation controllers to full smart home integration with energy monitoring.

  • Drip irrigation & greenhouse automation
  • Smart home (lighting, curtains, HVAC)
  • Commercial kitchen automation
  • Energy monitoring & management

Technical Consultancy

Independent technical advisory: feasibility studies, factory layout design, cost estimation, project planning, and bid evaluation — helping clients make informed decisions.

  • Feasibility studies & technical audits
  • Factory & production line layout
  • Cost estimation & ROI analysis
  • Project management & supervision

Hospitality Projects

Complete technology delivery for high-end hospitality: building management systems, security, audio/visual, power, and data distribution — including Sigiriya and Trincomalee projects.

  • BMS (HVAC, lighting, energy)
  • CCTV & access control
  • Home theatre & multi-room audio
  • Power & data backbone

02

03
