Who We
Are

NEXWAVE Solutions (Pvt) Ltd is a Colombo-based industrial technology company with deep expertise in automation, electronics, infrastructure, and smart systems. We design, build, and maintain the systems that power modern industry — and we've been doing it since 2017.

Our Story

Built by Engineers,
Driven by Purpose

We started with a simple idea: bring world-class industrial technology to Sri Lankan businesses, without the complexity and markup of multinational vendors. Eight years later, we've delivered 150+ projects across 12 industries.

2017

Founded

NEXWAVE started as a two-person automation consultancy in Colombo.

2019

First Major Contract

Delivered SCADA system for a national textile manufacturer — still running today.

2021

Expansion

Opened electronics division. Hired 20+ engineers. Added PCB assembly capability.

2023

Smart Systems & Hospitality

Launched agri automation, smart home, and high-end hospitality project divisions.

2025

Today

48 engineers, 150+ projects, 8 divisions — and still growing.
